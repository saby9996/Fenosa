Fenny

Fenny is the Conversation AI platform of the Fenosa ecosystem. Fenny assists the users by having a conversation with them regarding their legal needs. Prospectively, based on the conversation Fenny understands the case scenario of the person and further answers the legal questions related to the case.

  • Dynamic Conversational AI in Korean and English.
  • Litigation procedure support.

FenBook

FenBook is the client management system for lawyers, attorneys, and legal professionals. FenBook also provides the interface for its clients to find a lawyer and book online instantly to get legal advice anywhere, any time.

  • Easy Online Booking
  • VoIP and Video Conferencing Support with prospective Lawyers

FenReview

FenReview is a Contract or agreement validation platform when they are within predefined policies. But if the contract or agreement is not within the predefined policies then Fenosa’s Artificial Intelligence provides suggestions and recommendations for editing the contract or agreement.

  • Seamless Contract Review
  • AI that reads the document and presents recommendations if the agreements are not within the legal policies.

FenSign

FenSign is a cloud-based e-signing and contract lifecycle management system. The system allows a secured communication for the parties for mandating the agreements and digitally signing it anywhere, any time. Also, the system automates and connects the entire agreement process.

  • Industry and Department specific Solutions
  • Digital ID Verification for the Signee
