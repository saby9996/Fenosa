Fenny

Fenny is the Conversation AI platform of the Fenosa ecosystem. Fenny assists the users by having a conversation with them regarding their legal needs. Prospectively, based on the conversation Fenny understands the case scenario of the person and further answers the legal questions related to the case.

  • Dynamic Conversational AI in Korean and English
  • Case Type identification and understanding
  • Litigation procedure support
  • Lawyer and Attorney recommendation
  • Case Study Summarization and FenBook integration
  • One-Stop Solution for all the Legal related questions
  • Automated Lawyer appointment booking based upon the conversation
